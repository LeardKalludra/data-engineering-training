# Day 5 - Friday Data Sprint

## Business Scenario
We analyzed an e-commerce transaction dataset (`orders.csv`) containing order statuses, client details, item categories, locations, quantities, and prices. The goal of this sprint was to process the raw transactional records, separate completed sales from pending or cancelled orders, identify peak performers, and present clean, actionable financial metrics to a business owner.
